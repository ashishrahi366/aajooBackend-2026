AAJOO PRIVACY POLICY

Company: Aajoo Homes Private Limited (“Aajoo”, “we”, “us”, or “our”)
Applicable For: Aajoo App Users and Hosts

1. INTRODUCTION

Aajoo respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Aajoo app or website — whether as a User (guest/traveler) or a Host (property owner).

By using Aajoo, you agree to this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection and use of your data as described below.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

For Users (Guests)

  • Name, phone number, and email ID
  • Location data (for nearby property recommendations)
  • Payment information (processed via secure third-party gateways)
  • Booking history and preferences
  • Reviews and feedback

For Hosts

  • Full name, phone number, and email ID
  • Property details, photos, pricing, and availability
  • Identity verification (Aadhaar, PAN, or business registration)
  • Bank account details for payouts
  • KYC documents (as per Tourism Department guidelines)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device details (model, OS version, app usage logs)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Cookies and analytics (to improve user experience)

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • Create and manage your Aajoo account
  • Process bookings and payments
  • Verify host identities and property ownership
  • Provide customer support
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform safety
  • Share verified data with government authorities if required by law
  • Improve app features and personalize your experience

4. DATA SHARING & THIRD PARTIES

We do not sell or rent your data. We may share limited data with:

  • Payment gateways (for secure transactions)
  • Verification agencies (for KYC/document checks)
  • Tourism departments (for host registration compliance)
  • Analytics or marketing partners (to improve service performance)

All third parties follow strict data protection rules.

5. DATA STORAGE & SECURITY

  • Data is stored on secure, encrypted servers (AWS/Google Cloud – India Region).
  • Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • We use SSL encryption, firewalls, and periodic audits to prevent unauthorized access.
  • In case of any data breach, users and authorities will be notified as per the DPDP Act 2023.

6. USER RIGHTS

Under the DPDP Act, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored with us
  • Request corrections or updates
  • Withdraw consent or delete your account anytime
  • Know how your data is processed

To exercise these rights, email us at contactus@aajoohomes.com.

7. ACCOUNT DELETION

Users or Hosts can delete their accounts through the app or by contacting our support team. Once deleted:

  • All personal data and KYC details are erased within 7 days (except where required by law).
  • Transactional and tax-related data may be retained for 3 years for legal compliance.

8. COOKIES POLICY

We use cookies to:

  • Remember login sessions
  • Improve recommendations
  • Track analytics (anonymously)

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted in the app and on our website with the “Last Updated” date.
