AAJOO ACCOUNT DELETION POLICY

Company: Aajoo Homes Private Limited (“Aajoo”, “we”, “us”, or “our”)
Applicable For: Aajoo App Users and Hosts

1. Right to Delete Your Account

You have full control over your Aajoo account. You can request to permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time.

2. How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account by:

  • Going to Profile → Settings → Delete Account, or
  • Sending an email request to contactus@aajoohomes.com with your registered email and subject line “Account Deletion Request.”

Once your request is verified, your account will be deleted within 7 working days.

3. What Happens After Deletion

  • Your personal information (name, email, phone number, ID proofs, etc.) will be permanently removed from Aajoo’s active systems.
  • Booking history and payment records may be retained for legal and tax purposes as required by Indian law (for up to 180 days).
  • Any ongoing bookings will be automatically canceled as per the host’s cancellation policy.

4. Third-Party Data

If you’ve made payments through gateways (like Razorpay, Paytm, or UPI), those services may retain transaction records per RBI guidelines. Aajoo has no control over their data retention.

5. Reactivation

Once deleted, your account cannot be restored. You’ll need to create a new account if you wish to use Aajoo again.

6. Contact for Queries

If you face any issue with deletion or have privacy-related concerns, contact our support team:
📧 contactus@aajoohomes.com

✅ In Short:
You can delete your Aajoo account anytime. Your data will be removed securely within 7 days — with limited retention only where legally required.
